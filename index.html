Increasing efficiency of global store rollouts

Industry

One of the largest global coffee-house chains wanted to build a digital community to inspire designers and improve store rollout efficiency. The stores needed to be consistent with the brand whilst reflecting unique local ethos. Moonraft stepped in to design an immersive digital community that encouraged the cross-pollination of ideas and information amongst global store designers. This resulted in a single source of design inspiration & guidelines for new stores with a ‘global yet local’ experience.
